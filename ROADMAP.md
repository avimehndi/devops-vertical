# DevOps Vertical — High-Level Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the long-term development of the DevOps vertical at ColoredCow, focusing on capability building, service readiness, marketing assets, and sales enablement.

---

## **Phase 1 — Foundations (0–3 Months)**

- Establish core documentation and public assets  
- Publish initial public case studies  
- Finalize the evaluation assignment and candidate guidelines  
- Set up the public project board  
- Define role expectations for DevOps engineers  
- Build the hiring pipeline  
- Begin initial marketing alignment

---

## **Phase 2 — Capability Building (3–6 Months)**

- Create internal DevOps playbooks  
- Standardize migration processes  
- Build baseline CI/CD templates  
- Publish additional case studies  
- Create demo environments showcasing our expertise  
- Begin automating internal workflows  
- Develop marketing assets (web copy, social content plan, email templates)

---

## **Phase 3 — Service Offering Launch (6–12 Months)**

- Release a complete set of DevOps service offerings  
- Launch DevOps website pages  
- Create sales messaging and supporting assets  
- Begin outreach to Digital Agencies & Social Sector organizations  
- Create internal DevOps/SRE certification pathways  
- Standardize reusable frameworks & accelerators

---

## **Phase 4 — Growth & Optimization (12+ Months)**

- Publish 8–12 authoritative case studies  
- Expand services to DevSecOps, SRE, CloudOps, Observability  
- Build a strong inbound marketing funnel  
- Establish a partnership program  
- Track financial ROI and delivery outcomes  
- Build a community & thought-leadership pipeline

---

## **Roadmap Status Tracking**

For execution details, milestones, and current work items, see the **DevOps Capability Development** public project board.
